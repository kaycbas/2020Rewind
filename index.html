Overview:

2020 Rewind is an interactive map of the top trending daily Google searches for every US state in 2020.

Instructions:

Drag the slider to traverse 2020's searches, or click the play button to watch it like a movie. Enjoy!

Sources

Trending searches were gathered from Google's Year in Search summary and Daily Trending Searches, with help from the Wayback Machine.

Relative daily trends across states were determined using the Google Trends relative search interest tool, with help from the third party google-trends-api.
